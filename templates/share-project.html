The old EarSketch social sharing site will shut down on October 1, 2015. Here is the new way to share scripts in EarSketch:

  1. Save your script to the cloud using the Options menu.
  2. Open the scripts browser by clicking the scripts browser icon (green icon in top left of window).
  3. Click the share icon next to the script you want to share.
  4. Copy and paste the sharing URL to share via e-mail, social media sites, learning management systems like D2L or Google Classroom, or anywhere else you want.
  5. When someone opens the sharing URL, they'll see your code and music directly within EarSketch.
